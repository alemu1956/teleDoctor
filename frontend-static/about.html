About MalifoClinic

Delivering healthcare innovation across Ethiopia and beyond.

Who We Are

MalifoClinic is a pioneering healthcare platform committed to bridging the gap between patients and healthcare providers in Ethiopia. We empower patients to take control of their health by providing secure, decentralized medical records powered by blockchain technology.

Integrated with Ethiopia's National ID system (Fayda ID), MalifoClinic ensures patient data is safe, accessible, and fully compliant with national digital identity standards.

Our Mission

To make healthcare more accessible, secure, and efficient by using the latest technologies, and to connect every Ethiopian to quality health services regardless of their location.

Why Choose MalifoClinic?

Immutable and decentralized record-keeping for secure health data.

Seamless identity verification for patients and doctors.

Accessible Healthcare

Online consultations and record access anytime, anywhere.
